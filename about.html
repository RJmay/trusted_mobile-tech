Our story

Trusted Mobile Tech started in 2022 with a simple frustration: too many people on the Sunshine Coast were being told a cracked screen or tired battery meant buying a new device, when a careful technician could have the original fixed the same day. So we set up in Buderim QLD 4556 and got to work, repairing screens, batteries, charging ports and everything in between for neighbours, tradies, students and small businesses.

Word spread the way it should. Customers came back with a partner's iPad, a kid's iPhone, the office MacBook, and they told their friends why. Today we look after the whole Sunshine Coast, from Caloundra to Maroochydore and Sippy Downs, and run regular mobile pickup routes down to Brisbane. We are still fully independent, still Australian owned, and every repair still gets the same care as the very first one. You can also find us inside the main site at trustedmobiletech.org.
