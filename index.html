📋 How to find your device info ▼
Model (iPhone / iPad)
Go to Settings → General → About. Look for Model Name (e.g. iPhone 14 Pro) and Capacity (e.g. 256 GB).
Model (MacBook)
Click the Apple menu → About This Mac. You'll see the chip (e.g. M2), year, and storage.
Battery Health
On iPhone/iPad: Settings → Battery → Battery Health & Charging. On MacBook: hold Option and click the battery icon in the menu bar.
IMEI Number (iPhone / iPad)
Go to Settings → General → About → IMEI. Or dial *#06# on the phone app. You can also find it on the original box. Providing your IMEI helps us verify the device and give you a more accurate quote.

Trusted Mobile Tech was founded in 2022 on the Sunshine Coast with a clear mission: deliver professional-grade device repairs at fair prices, without the corporate runaround. What began as a local operation quickly earned a reputation for reliability, transparency, and quality workmanship.

Word spread fast across the Coast because we do things differently — honest pricing, genuine warranties, and technicians who actually stand behind their work. Three years and hundreds of satisfied customers later, we're proud to be the repair service locals recommend to their families.
