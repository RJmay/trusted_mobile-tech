Common iPad Faults We Repair

Every iPad generation is built a little differently, so the right fix depends on the exact model on the bench. Here is what arrives at our Buderim workshop most often.

Cracked glass & digitiser

On classic iPads and older minis, the touch layer is a separate panel sitting over the LCD. If the glass is smashed but the picture underneath is still perfect, we can usually replace just that layer, keeping the job simpler and the turnaround quick.

Full laminated displays

iPad Air and iPad Pro models fuse glass, touch and LCD into one laminated unit, so damage to any layer means replacing the whole assembly. We fit quality panels and verify touch accuracy, colour and brightness before handover.

Battery replacement

iPad batteries are large, glued down and unforgiving to remove. If yours drains overnight, shuts down mid-lesson or is starting to bulge, we open the chassis safely, swap the cell and reseal the housing properly.

Charging port faults

Lint, bent pins and worn Lightning or USB-C ports are the usual suspects when an iPad charges only at odd angles or not at all. We clean the port first, test it under load, and replace it only if it genuinely needs it.

Water damage

Poolside drops and spilled drinks happen. We strip the device down, clean corrosion from the board and assess what is recoverable. If we cannot bring it back, you pay nothing: that is our no-fix-no-fee promise.

Home & power buttons

A sticky Home button or a dead power button makes an otherwise healthy iPad infuriating. We repair the flex cables and switches behind them, and we will explain the Touch ID pairing limits honestly before any work begins.

Kid-Proofing the Family iPad

Plenty of the iPads we see were in a small pair of hands moments before the accident. A little prevention goes a long way: a tempered glass protector absorbs the impact that would otherwise crack the digitiser, and a chunky rubber case with raised corners protects the aluminium body on tiled Queensland floors. Ask us when you collect your repair and we will happily advise on the best combo for your model.

iPad Repair FAQs

Does my iPad need just the glass, or the whole display?

It depends on the model. Standard iPads and older minis use a separate glass digitiser, so a cracked front with a healthy picture often needs only the top layer. Air and Pro models use a single laminated assembly, so the full display is replaced. Tell us your model in the online quote and we will confirm which applies.

My iPad only charges when the cable sits at a weird angle. Is the port dead?

Not always. Compressed lint at the bottom of the port causes this more often than a failed connector, and a proper clean fixes many of them on the spot. If the pins are worn or bent we replace the port itself, and we test charging under load before it leaves the bench.

Will a Home button repair break Touch ID?

On iPads with Touch ID, the fingerprint sensor is paired to the logic board, so a third-party replacement button restores clicking but not fingerprint unlock. Where possible we repair your original button to preserve Touch ID, and we will always explain the options before starting.

How long does an iPad battery replacement take?

Most iPad battery swaps are completed the same day. The adhesive holding iPad batteries takes patience to release safely, so we allow a few hours to do it without stressing the display or the frame. Book in the morning and it is usually ready by the afternoon.
