Common iPhone faults we fix

If your fault is not listed here, ask anyway. If we cannot repair it, you pay nothing.

Cracked screens and back glass

A drop on the driveway does not have to mean a new phone. We replace shattered front screens and rear glass panels, restoring full touch response, True Tone and that fresh-out-of-the-box feel.

Battery health and replacement

If Battery Health has slipped low or the phone shuts down at 30 percent, a fresh battery brings back genuine all-day life. We check performance before and after the swap so you can see the difference.

Charging port cleaning and replacement

Beach sand, pocket lint and salt air love Lightning and USB-C ports. Often a careful professional clean cures a loose or fussy cable. When the port itself has failed, we replace it properly.

Camera faults

Blurry shots, a rattling lens or a black viewfinder usually point to a failed camera module. We fit replacements and verify focus, stabilisation and flash on both front and rear cameras.

Water damage

Dropped it in the pool or copped a wave at Mooloolaba? Power it off and get it to us fast. We open the phone, treat corrosion and give you an honest assessment before any work begins.

Face ID issues

Face ID needs careful diagnosis because its sensors are paired to your phone. We troubleshoot dot projector, flood illuminator and front camera faults and tell you straight what can be saved.

iPhone repair questions, answered

Will Face ID still work after a screen replacement?

Yes. We carefully transfer the original sensor assembly from your old screen to the new one, so Face ID, the proximity sensor and auto-brightness keep working exactly as before.

Is it worth replacing the battery in an older iPhone?

Usually, yes. If the phone otherwise runs well, a new battery is far cheaper than an upgrade and typically adds a year or two of comfortable use. Grab an instant online quote and decide for yourself.

My iPhone got wet but seems fine. Should I still bring it in?

We recommend it. Salt water and pool chlorine keep corroding the board for days after the dunk, and phones that "seemed fine" often fail weeks later. A quick inspection and clean now can save the phone.

Can you replace just the back glass without a full housing swap?

On most recent models, yes. We remove the shattered rear glass and fit a new panel without replacing the whole frame, which keeps the job faster and more affordable than a full housing change.
