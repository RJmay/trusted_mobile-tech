Refurbished device questions, answered

What does the 90-day warranty cover?

Hardware faults. If something stops working within 90 days, bring it back to Buderim or arrange a pickup and we make it right. Cosmetic wear described in the grade, and accidental damage after purchase, are not covered.

How do I know the device is not stolen or locked?

Before listing, we check the IMEI or serial number and confirm activation lock has been removed. You sign in with your own Apple ID on day one, with a clean history behind the device.

Is refurbished the same as second-hand?

Not quite. A private second-hand sale is as-is with no comeback. Our refurbished devices are tested, sanitised, graded and warrantied, and we are still here next week if you need us.

Can I trade in my current device?

Yes. We buy used phones, MacBooks and iPads for cash, which can take a healthy chunk off the price of your next device. See our sell your device page or mention it when you call.
